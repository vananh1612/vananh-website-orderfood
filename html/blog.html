Seafood fried rice known as seafood fried rice in English, is a delectable fusion of flavors. Tender shrimp, succulent squid, and delicate fish are sautéed with fragrant jasmine rice, creating a symphony of tastes. Enhanced by a medley of fresh vegetables and savory seasonings, this dish is a delightful harmony of textures and aromas, capturing the essence of oceanic indulgence.


Pumpkin dessert soup, also known as "chè bí ngô" in Vietnamese, is a popular Vietnamese sweet treat made from cooked pumpkin, coconut milk, and various toppings. It offers a creamy texture, subtle sweetness, and a delightful combination of flavors that is enjoyed both hot and cold.


Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish consisting of vinegared rice combined with fresh fish, seafood, or vegetables, wrapped in seaweed. It is known for its exquisite taste, beautiful presentation, and the art of sushi-making.
